Turkish Rakı

what is a Rakı?

Rakı or raki is an alcoholic drink made of twice-distilled grapes. It is the national drink of Turkey. It is often served with seafood or meze. Due to the aniseed it contains, raki changes color and becomes a milky white when water is added and a glass of pure water to go with it gives a distinct pleasant taste.
